Rabbit's Book Adventure

Rabbit's Book Adventure is a project from one of my subject. We were required to make a game for a company of out choice and so we picked GrandDude Studio and with the permission of the owner we made it.

Top-Down Apple Picker

Its a two player game that requires players to pick up apples to win but there is a twist. each player can bump the other player to knock them back and hit the bushes which stuns the player.

Push n' Carry

Inspired by Fireboy and Watergirl. It is a two player puzzle game that requires players to get the keys to get to the next level. The boy can only pick up boxes while the girl can only push the boxes.

Cat vs Cat

A two player game that lets players play as cat and detroy the other players cat bed but dont just shoot as the players can damage their own bed.
